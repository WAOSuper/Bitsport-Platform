Why provide LP?

When you provide LP to the Bitsport trading pools, you are directly helping the project grow as well as your investment. Having a larger liquidity pool gives confidence to larger buyers to make purchases.

Earn twice!

When fueling the thruster with your LP tokens you are automatically subject to receiving a portion of all trading fees. Hooray for DeFi!
When staking those LP tokens in the Thrusters, you receive extra BTP rewards for your commitment.

Decentralize all the things!

Bisport is committed to creating the decentralized environment we believe will ultimately bring the most value to all of our users.
By committing tokens as LP we spread the holders out and create a robust pool of tokens owned by many individuals.
Bitsport is also refining an LP buyback system to permanently lock a growing amount of LP to the pools.

BitSport Creatives Initiative

BitSport wants you!

BitSport believes that creatives have not been given their dues for too long, and commercial work constraints have limited their scopes only to underpay in a market with limited opportunities.
BitSport believes we can do better, and then continue getting better from there. The BitSport Knock-Out Universe will be created by the BitSport users. Media contributions will be open to the public and voted on by users.
Winning contributions will be paid a one time payment in BTP and be allotted a portion of the royalties of the NFT from then onwards. BitSport at its core has been developed as a platform to help its users grow, and will continue to progress in that mission.
